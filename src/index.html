About Cogniflex

Cogniflex is an interactive platform focused on stimulating and developing cognitive flexibility and social adaptation skills for individuals on the Autism Spectrum (ASD).

How does it work?

We offer a safe, playful, and controlled environment where you can experience simulations of real everyday situations. In each scenario, you will make decisions through several choice points and will be able to observe the practical, social, and emotional consequences of your approaches.

Our stories feature multiple alternatives that go beyond the rigid concept of "right or wrong," encouraging the perception of nuances and shades of gray in human interactions.
